**Basic Structures of Matter: Supergravitation Unified Theory**
//by Stoyan Sarg// 

Main book about the theory. Target audience are scientists and everybody capable of reading a scientific paper. The book is understandable and does not require higher math skills. It is highly suggested to read the book in chronological order.
